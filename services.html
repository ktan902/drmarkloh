Services

Dr Mark Loh attends to newborn deliveries and paediatric emergencies at Thomson Medical Centre, Mt Alvernia Hospital, Gleneagles, Mt Elizabeth (Orchard) and Mt Elizabeth (Novena) Hospitals.

Neonatology:


Dr Loh specializes in providing newborn intensive care (NICU) in the various hospitals, especially for the very premature and sick infants.

Paediatric Medicine:


His clinic at Mt Elizabeth (Novena) Specialist Centre provides a wide range of services geared towards primary care of all children. This includes a surveillance program of regular growth and developmental checks, and detailed childhood immunizations for well children. Comprehensive medical care for acutely unwell children begins in his well-equipped clinic staffed by experienced nurses, and can be extended to hospital in-patient management, if the need arises.
